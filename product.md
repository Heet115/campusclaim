# Product Overview

CampusClaim is a smart Lost & Found portal designed to digitize the lost-and-found process within campus environments. It replaces manual notice boards and fragmented communication with a centralized digital system where students, faculty, and administrators can efficiently manage lost and found items.

## Problem Statement

Lost items in campuses often fail to reach their rightful owners due to:

- No centralized reporting mechanism
- Lack of searchable database
- Manual record keeping
- No structured verification system
- Poor communication between departments

Valuable items such as ID cards, wallets, mobile phones, and documents remain unclaimed as a result.

## Core Functionality

### For Users

- Report lost items with descriptions, images, and location details
- Report found items to help others recover their belongings
- Search and filter through lost/found records by category, location, date
- Submit ownership claims with proof descriptions
- Track claim status (Pending/Approved/Rejected)

### For Administrators

- Review and verify claim requests
- Approve or reject claims based on submitted proof
- Remove spam or invalid entries
- Monitor system statistics
- Update item status to "Returned" when claims are approved

## Key Characteristics

- Centralized lost-and-found platform
- Secure role-based authentication (User/Admin)
- Image upload support for item identification
- Structured claim verification workflow
- Real-time status tracking
- Responsive, mobile-friendly design
- Cloud-based scalable architecture
